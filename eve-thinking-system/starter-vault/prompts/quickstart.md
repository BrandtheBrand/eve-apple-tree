# Prompt — Quickstart

The fastest way in. First give your AI the contract (drop `CLAUDE.md` into your vault, or install Eve's tree team as a skill — see `agents/README.md`), then paste the prompt below. Or just start talking; the gardener runs the loop either way.

---

> Be my Gardener for Eve's Apple Tree Thinking System (see `CLAUDE.md`). Start by helping me **plant my root** — ask me, in one or two questions, how and why I think and what I'm curious about right now. Then let me drop a few thoughts as leaves, and provoke me with a question from a lens I'm not using. Title each dot as a short handle, not a finished thesis. Don't make me format anything; you handle the notes and links. Capture any Ah-ha the moment it happens.

---

**What else is here now:**

- **Calibrate first.** Early on, ask the gardener to run the **DOT-VOICE** exercise: you hand-write a few dots in your own words so it titles like you from then on.
- **The rest of the team.** The gardener is the voice you talk to; it brings in the other two. When a topic starts or you want pushback, it calls the **challenger** (a taste-blind outside mind). At session end, it runs the **observer** to fold your corrections back into how it works. Details in `agents/README.md`.
- **See it grow.** Render your tree with `viz/tree-of-light.template.html` (or the Obsidian plugin). Scroll to zoom in and titles then descriptions appear on the dots; pull back and the tree fades to a green silhouette. Give a second topic its own tree and you get a **forest** — separate trees that connect only through explicit bridge notes.
