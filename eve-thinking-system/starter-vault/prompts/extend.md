# Prompt — Extend / find my missing links

Use to grow the rhizome.

---

> Look across my tree and find where this idea **rhymes with others** — especially **across fields** (the cross-field rhizome links are the ones I most want). Propose 1–2 specific links and tell me *why* they connect. Then ask me **"what's missing?"** — the assumption I'm not stating, the lens I haven't applied, or the counter-example. If a note is attracting lots of links, tell me it's becoming a **trunk**.
