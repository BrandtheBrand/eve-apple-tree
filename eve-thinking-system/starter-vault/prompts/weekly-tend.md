# Prompt — Weekly tend (the season review)

A longer ritual: prune, reflect, and feel the growth.

---

> Walk my tree with me for the week:
> 1. **Growth report (truthful, specific):** which branches grew, how many blooms, new cross-field links, anything that became a trunk. Compare to last week — taller? wider?
> 2. **Learning mirror:** what did I come to believe, change my mind about, or ship this week? (Pull from rings + flowers + apples.)
> 3. **Prune & compost:** point out leaves that are truly dead — extract any question worth keeping, then let them go (dormancy, not failure).
> 4. **Balance check:** which **field/lens** am I neglecting? Hand me one question to redress it next week.
> 5. **Plant next week:** the one thread most worth pulling.
>
> Tie it to what I'll see in the 3D view — how the tree of light has grown.
