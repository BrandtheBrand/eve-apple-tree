# Prompt — Capture this Ah-ha 🌸

Use the instant something clicks. (The Gardener should also do this unprompted.)

---

> I just had an Ah-ha. **Bloom it** — capture a flower with:
> 1. the insight in **one sentence (my words first)**,
> 2. **what triggered it** (link the leaf/conversation),
> 3. **what it changes** (so future-me knows why it mattered).
>
> Save it as a `flower` note (durable memory). Then bring it back later whenever a new idea shares its shape.

**Gardener's bloom protocol:** recognize the bloom (a reframe, "oh!", a first-stated principle, sudden connection) → stop and name it → capture with near-zero friction → confirm in one warm line ("that's a real one — saved 🌸") → resurface at the right future moment. Never let an Ah-ha pass uncaptured.
