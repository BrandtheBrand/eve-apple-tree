title: What am I avoiding feeling?
---

When I put a task off, what am I actually stepping away from — the task, or a feeling the task brings up? Is it boredom, or the fear of doing it badly? What would I notice if I watched the exact moment I flinch away? What kind of evidence would even tell me the difference?
