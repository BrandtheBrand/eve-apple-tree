## 🌱 Root — how & why I think

I think best when I stop trying to sort ideas and start letting them collide. I trust a connection more than a category, I get honest when I have to say a thing out loud, and I learn fastest when I make something small instead of planning something big. My four lenses: Researcher, Maker, Critic, Storyteller.

## My fields (lenses)
- Researcher — what would count as evidence, what would change my mind
- Maker — the smallest version I could build to find out
- Critic — the weakest link, who pays, the second-order effect
- Storyteller — who it's for, the one-sentence version, the metaphor
