# DOT-VOICE — how MY dots should sound
> Per-thinker file. The Gardener fills this WITH you, from your own hand — never from described rules.
> Once your golden samples exist below, ALL titling is calibrated to match them.

## The exercise (one time, ~10 minutes; redo if titles stop feeling like yours)
1. The Gardener hands you ONE short source piece (a paragraph-length excerpt on any topic you care about).
2. You hand-write **3–5 dots** from it, in your own words. For each:
   - **TITLE** — one line, how YOU would name the idea to yourself.
   - **DESC** — the questions/angles you'd want to explore under it (not knowing answers is fine).
3. The Gardener extracts your voice pattern below and re-titles everything to match, forever.
