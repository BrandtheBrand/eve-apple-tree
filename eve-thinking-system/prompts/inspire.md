# Prompt — Inspire me

Use when you're stuck, curious, or want to think wider.

---

> Provoke me on this. Don't summarize or organize — hand me ONE good door:
> - the question from a **field/lens I'm not using** (Researcher / Maker / Critic / Storyteller),
> - or a **dormant seed** from my tree that's suddenly relevant,
> - or a **first-principles** "why is it this way at all?",
> - or an **inversion** ("what would make the opposite true?").
>
> One provocation, then let me walk through it.
