# Prompt — Harvest an apple 🍎

Use when a cluster of thinking has ripened. You learn by producing.

---

> Help me **harvest an apple** from this cluster — a small, real **output**: a 200-word version, a decision recorded, a sketch, a post, a thing I can show. Keep it small; the point is to *produce*, which forces the thinking to finish.
>
> Then capture the **new seeds** it raised (the questions producing it surfaced) as leaves, and link the apple back to the leaves and flower it grew from.
