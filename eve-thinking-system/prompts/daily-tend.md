# Prompt — Daily tend (5 minutes)

A short ritual to keep the tree alive.

---

> Tend my tree for five minutes:
> 1. **Resurface** one or two notes I haven't touched lately — are they still alive? Did one grow a **ring** (do I understand it better now)?
> 2. **One provocation** tied to what I was last thinking about.
> 3. If I bloomed or shipped today, **name the growth** specifically (and truthfully).
>
> Keep it light. End with the single most interesting open question on my tree right now.
