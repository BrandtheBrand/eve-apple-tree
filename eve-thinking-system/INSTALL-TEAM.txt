EVE'S TREE TEAM (AI companion kit) — add the team to a vault you already have
==============================================================================

1. Copy EVERYTHING inside this eve-tree-team/ folder into your vault's ROOT
   folder (the folder you open in Obsidian). Include the hidden .claude/ folder.

   Already have a CLAUDE.md in your vault? Do NOT overwrite it — open both and
   merge by hand (this one is the team's operating contract).

2. Open the vault folder in Claude Code and say: gardener, let's think
   The first session runs a short voice-calibration exercise.

3. No Claude Code? Paste the body of .claude/skills/gardener/SKILL.md into any
   capable AI as its system prompt. Run the challenger in a separate, fresh
   conversation (no memory features), and the observer in another.

The templates/ folder holds note templates (root, trunk, leaf, flower, apple,
field, bridge) if you prefer to grow the tree fully by hand. The team proposes,
you decide: it never creates your flowers or apples, and writes nothing without
your approval.
